Tacos

CONDADO

"We're all about the tacos, tequila + margaritas but the main attraction is our build-your-own tacos!"

Large murals combined with a fun and young atmosphere make for a great experience. While not the most authentic, the customization options and size of the nachos more than make up for it!

971 Liberty Ave Pittsburgh, PA 15222

4300 Butler St Pittsburgh, PA 15201

301 South Hills Village Space #2400 Pittsburgh, PA 15241

BAKERSFIELD

"Serving up authentic, Mexican street fare, and a selection of over 100 tequilas and American whiskeys. Bakersfield is a spot where the food is simple, the service is warm and the atmosphere is alive."

Drinks and tacos? Say no more. They have a very modern and fun atmosphere. Margarita pitchers are a must when you come here.

940 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

DOCE TAQUERIA

"Taco specialist turning out pork, beef, chicken & vegan bites in a compact, vibrantly colored space."

The tacos here are a bit bigger than most other places. Great value for your money. If you're looking for takeout tacos look no further.

1302 E Carson St, Pittsburgh, PA 15203

4826 McKnight Road, Pittsburgh, PA 15237
