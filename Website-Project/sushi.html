SUSHI TOO

"Cooked Japanese fare & varied sushi rolls are offered in compact, basic digs with a full bar."

Not too pricey and also has other foods besides sushi including katsu don,noodles, fried rice and more.

5432 Walnut St Pittsburgh, PA 15232

SUSHI BOMB

Roomy, popular destination for all-you-can-eat Japanese food, including sushi & entrees

Very good value for what you get. All you can eat sushi may seem a bit off-putting but it is better than you think. The gyoza are to die for! you can even leave and go on a walk and come back for more.

6302 Robinson Centre Dr, Pittsburgh, PA 15205

MOLA

Easygoing, contemporary locale fixing up sushi plates, rice bowls & bubble teas.

Modern, casual, but nice dining. Great atmosphere plus great food what more could you ask for? Extensive choice of drinks and sushi make this a must try for sushi lovers!

6018 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206
